Welcome to Beenzer dApp

OWN YOUR CONTENT

Mint NFTs with your Smartphone Camera

With Beenzer, users can take photos or videos with their phone and turn them into one-of-a-kind NFTs that they can keep, share, or even sell to their fans.

This gives users complete control over their own content and allows them to monetize their creations in ways that were previously impossible.

Unlike traditional social media platforms, Beenzer puts the power back in the hands of the users, rather than big centralized tech companies.

Amazing Features

CONNECT YOUR WALLET

Beenzer is a truly decentralized app. We don't store your media on centralized servers. Instead, your NFTs are stored on the Solana blockchain, ensuring true ownership and immutability of your digital content.

CREATE & TRADE BEENZERS

Create unique NFTs directly from your smartphone camera. Upload photos, videos, or audio and mint them as Solana-based tokens with limited supply. Trade your Beenzers on our marketplace or hold them as digital collectibles.

EXPLORE THE MAP

Discover geo-located NFTs around the world. Browse the interactive map to find Beenzers dropped by users near you. Explore unique digital collectibles tied to real-world locations and expand your NFT collection.
