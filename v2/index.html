About Beenzer

OWN YOUR CONTENT

Mint NFTs with your phone camera

üöÄ Beenzer is a revolutionary new app that allows users to create and mint their own unique digital collectibles, called NFTs, using the power of blockchain technology.

With Beenzer, users can take photos or videos with their phone and turn them into one-of-a-kind NFTs that they can keep, share, or even sell to their fans. This gives users complete control over their own content and allows them to monetize their creations in ways that were previously impossible.

Unlike traditional social media platforms, Beenzer puts the power back in the hands of the users, rather than big centralized tech companies.
